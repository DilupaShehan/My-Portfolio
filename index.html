SLUG Kabaddi Champions 2023

Highlighting the Historic Victory & Best Player Award

A Historic Milestone for USJ Kabaddi

2023 marked a significant year for the University of Sri Jayewardenepura as we introduced Kabaddi to the Sri Lanka University Games (SLUG). It was a debut that resonated through the sports arena, culminating in a dominant performance where we emerged as the Champions.

Beyond the team victory, I am deeply honored to have been recognized as the Best Player of the championship-winning team. This recognition was further celebrated at the prestigious J'pura Colours Night, where I was awarded USJ Colours for outstanding performance and leadership on the mat.

This achievement represents the discipline, strategy, and relentless training that our team invested to put USJ at the pinnacle of university Kabaddi in Sri Lanka.

Inter-University Runners-Up 2024

SLUSA Colours Winner & Remarkable Team Performance

Competing with Pride at the Inter-University Games

In 2024, our team returned to the Inter-University arena with even greater determination. We fought through intense competition to secure the Runners-Up position, proving our consistency and skill at the national university level.

This year was particularly significant for me as it marked another milestone in my athletic career. I was honored to be named a SLUSA Colours Winner, a recognition given to the top-performing athletes across all Sri Lankan universities.

The journey to the finals was a testament to our team's growth, leadership, and the high standards we've set for J'pura Kabaddi. We competed with pride, and this achievement serves as a stepping stone towards our future goals.

SLUG XV Runners-Up 2025

Best Player, Best Raider & SLUSA Colours Winner

A Thrilling Final and Triple Individual Honors

The 2025 Sri Lanka University Games (SLUG XV) was a showcase of high-intensity Kabaddi. After a thriller final that kept spectators on the edge of their seats, our team secured the Runners-Up position in a hard-fought battle.

On a personal level, this tournament was a defining moment. I was incredibly honored to be awarded the Best Player of the Tournament and the Best Raider, recognizing my contributions on the offensive front during critical moments of the games.

Furthermore, I was once again recognized as a SLUSA Colours Winner, maintaining a standard of excellence in the national university sports arena. These achievements reflect the dedication to the sport and the relentless spirit of J'pura Kabaddi.
